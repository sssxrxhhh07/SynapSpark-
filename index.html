Musical Theory Behind the Grid
Safe zones (load < 75%) — Consonant sine waves tuned to C major intervals (C4=262Hz, E4=330Hz, G4=392Hz). Mathematically, consonance arises from simple frequency ratios (3:2, 5:4).
Warning zones (75–85%) — Slightly detuned oscillators with minor intervals (dissonance ratio ≈ 9:8, 16:15).
Danger zones (> 85%) — Sawtooth waves (rich in harmonics) tuned to diminished chords. Load % maps linearly to frequency: f = 200 + (load − 40) × 4 Hz.
